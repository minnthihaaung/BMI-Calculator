U Thant

The third Secretary-General of the United Nations

Here's the time line of U Thant's life:

  • 1909 - Born in Pantanaw, Myanmar
  • 1931 - Became the headmaster of the national schoool
  • 1942 - Served for a few months as Secretary of Burma's Education Reorganization Committee
  • 1947 - U Thant was appointed Press Director of the Government of Myanmar
  • 1961 - Serving as Acting Secretary-General
  • 1966 - U Thant was re-appointed for a second term as Secretary-General of the United Nations
  • 1974 - Died after a long illness

"As we watch sun goes down, evening after evening, through the smog across the poisoned waters of our native earth, we must ask ourselves seriously whether whether we really wish some future universial historian on another planet to say about us: With all their genius and with all their skill, they ran out of foresight and air and food and water and ideas. They went on playing politics until their world collapsed around them."

-- U Thant Secretary-General of United Nations
